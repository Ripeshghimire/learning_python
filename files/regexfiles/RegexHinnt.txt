. - Any Chracter except new line
\d - Any digit between (0-9)
\D - Not a digit
\w - Word character (a-z, A-Z,0-9,_)
\W - not a word character
\s - whitespace (space, tab,newline)
\S - not white space

Invisible postions
\b - Word Boundry -- Word Boundy = space tab etc
\B - Not a word Boundry
^ - Beginning of a string
$ - End of a string
[] = Matches Characters in brackets
[] = Matches Character not in brackers
| - Either or
() - Group

Quantifiers:
* - 0 or more
+ - 1 or more
? - 0 or one
{3} - exact number
{3,4} - Range of number
